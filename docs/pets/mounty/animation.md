Palette combines slate gray, ice blue hooves, and bright saffron climbing harness straps. Face: square jaw, keen jade eyes, and playful goatee; art style resembles inked expedition posters. Accessories: bandana map around the neck, miniature ice axe tucked into the harness, and floating altitude ticker. Positioned near the center with hooves gripping stylized cliff ledges carved into the dial.
