# StepSprite

Pet visual description: StepSprite resembles a mischievous pixel spirit made of stacked geometric blobs shaped like a running shoe silhouette. Palette mixes lime green, teal, and electric yellow with thin purple edge lines reminiscent of synthwave motion graphics. Face: wide rectangular eyes with LCD-like gradients, expressive eyebrows, and a mischievous smirk that stretches across the top module. Accessories include a luminous sweat scarf, ankle charm pedometer, and floating step rings that orbit the torso. Size: roughly two-thirds of the dial width, hovering just above the complication strip; body language perpetually in mid-stride.
