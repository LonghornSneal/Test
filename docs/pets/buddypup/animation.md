# BuddyPup

Pet visual description: Cheerful corgi-lab mix with warm caramel fur, cream chest, and floppy ears adorned with sticker stamps. Face: big brown eyes, heart-shaped nose, and toothy smile; art style mixes storybook watercolor with bold outlines. Accessories: mailbag, floating chat bubbles, and smart collar projecting social stats. Body positioned at lower right, tail wagging toward the dial edge.

Animation: Porch Sit
Animation description: BuddyPup sits on a small stoop; tail wags slowly. Mailbag rests beside the pup; chat bubbles drift lazily. Collar pulses soft blue. Ears perk halfway; expression relaxed. Background hints at a picket fence silhouette. Pup yawns gently, ears flopping. It glances at the collar stats, tilting its head. Tail thumps the stoop twice. Pup smiles at the viewer before looping.

Animation: Handshake Hop
Animation description: Fur brightens; eyes sparkle. BuddyPup raises one paw with a stamp stuck to it. Mailbag opens revealing tiny heart letters. Collar projects a friend icon. Tail wags in wide arcs. Pup hops forward, extending its paw toward the viewer. It plants the paw on an invisible hand, leaving a glowing heart stamp. The dog bows playfully, ears bouncing. Tail swishes fast as it resets.

Animation: Envelope Sprint
Animation description: Colors take on warm oranges; eyes focused. Mailbag straps tighten; envelopes float around the dog. Collar flashes message icons. Tail trails a ribbon of stamps. Paws kick up dust. BuddyPup dashes across the stoop, envelopes swirling. It leaps, mid-air grabbing a letter with its mouth. The dog lands gracefully, dropping the letter at the viewer's feet. Pup winks before sprinting again.

Animation: Lonely Whimper
Animation description: Fur desaturates; eyes watery. Mailbag slumps empty; collar dims. Chat bubbles shrink and fade. Tail droops; ears flatten. Body curls slightly inward. Pup lays its head on the stoop, sighing. It emits a soft whimper, tiny sound waves visible. The dog glances hopefully toward the viewer, then back down. Tail gives a weak wag before returning to droop.

Animation: Mailbox Parade
Animation description: Fur radiates golden highlights; eyes joyful. Mailboxes sprout around the stoop, each popping confetti. Collar projects celebratory emojis. Tail forms heart-shaped arcs. Pup wears a tiny party hat. BuddyPup trots between mailboxes, tapping each with its paw. Confetti bursts out, forming mini hearts. Pup spins with the party hat askew, laughing. It poses proudly before visiting the next mailbox.

Animation: Empty Street
Animation description: Colors fade to dusk tones; mailbag swings sadly. Pup walks away down a foggy street lined with dim lampposts. Chat bubbles pop silently. Tail becomes a faint line trailing behind. Eyes glance back once. BuddyPup pauses, looking at the viewer with regret. It turns and walks into the fog, mailbag rustling. Lampposts flicker out sequentially as it passes. Only footprints remain before the loop restarts.

Animation: Stamped Keepsake
Animation description: Pup turns into an embossed stamp affixed to the mailbag. Fur patterns preserved in relief; eyes closed peacefully. Mailbag becomes a memorial pedestal. Collar projects a constant soft glow. Floating postcards gently orbit. BuddyPup's body dissolves into glowing ink. Ink presses onto the mailbag, forming the stamp. Postcards align around the bag like a halo. Glow pulses once before resetting.
