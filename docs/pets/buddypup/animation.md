# CosmoBond BuddyPup Animation Plan — Gemini GIF Workflow

## Production Approach
- **Generator:** Google Gemini video diffusion capturing expressive canine facial animation with messenger props.
- **Export guardrails:** 438 × 438 px, 4-second loops, twilight plum (#0B0711) background, and three-quarter camera to fit mail routes.
- **Post-processing:** Convert to 60 fps PNG stacks, accentuate glow on message envelopes in After Effects, and ship GIF/WebM plus sprite sheets.

## Pet Model Snapshot
- Cheerful corgi-lab mix with warm caramel fur, cream chest, and floppy ears adorned with sticker stamps.
- Face: big brown eyes, heart-shaped nose, and toothy smile; art style mixes storybook watercolor with bold outlines.
- Accessories: mailbag, floating chat bubbles, and smart collar projecting social stats.
- Body positioned at lower right, tail wagging toward the dial edge.

## Interaction Map
- **Social Greet:** Triggered when the user replies to messages or logs meetups, showing excited mail delivery.
- **Message Delivery:** Fires when BuddyPup forwards notifications or shared postcards, highlighting courier poses.

## Animation States

### Idle State — "Porch Sit"
- **Title:** Porch Sit
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - BuddyPup sits on a small stoop; tail wags slowly.
  - Mailbag rests beside the pup; chat bubbles drift lazily.
  - Collar pulses soft blue.
  - Ears perk halfway; expression relaxed.
  - Background hints at a picket fence silhouette.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Pup yawns gently, ears flopping.
  2. It glances at the collar stats, tilting its head.
  3. Tail thumps the stoop twice.
  4. Pup smiles at the viewer before looping.

### Interaction State — Social Greet "Handshake Hop"
- **Title:** Handshake Hop
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Fur brightens; eyes sparkle.
  - BuddyPup raises one paw with a stamp stuck to it.
  - Mailbag opens revealing tiny heart letters.
  - Collar projects a friend icon.
  - Tail wags in wide arcs.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Pup hops forward, extending its paw toward the viewer.
  2. It plants the paw on an invisible hand, leaving a glowing heart stamp.
  3. The dog bows playfully, ears bouncing.
  4. Tail swishes fast as it resets.

### Interaction State — Message Delivery "Envelope Sprint"
- **Title:** Envelope Sprint
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Colors take on warm oranges; eyes focused.
  - Mailbag straps tighten; envelopes float around the dog.
  - Collar flashes message icons.
  - Tail trails a ribbon of stamps.
  - Paws kick up dust.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. BuddyPup dashes across the stoop, envelopes swirling.
  2. It leaps, mid-air grabbing a letter with its mouth.
  3. The dog lands gracefully, dropping the letter at the viewer’s feet.
  4. Pup winks before sprinting again.

### Sick State — "Lonely Whimper"
- **Title:** Lonely Whimper
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Fur desaturates; eyes watery.
  - Mailbag slumps empty; collar dims.
  - Chat bubbles shrink and fade.
  - Tail droops; ears flatten.
  - Body curls slightly inward.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Pup lays its head on the stoop, sighing.
  2. It emits a soft whimper, tiny sound waves visible.
  3. The dog glances hopefully toward the viewer, then back down.
  4. Tail gives a weak wag before returning to droop.

### Happy State — "Mailbox Parade"
- **Title:** Mailbox Parade
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Fur radiates golden highlights; eyes joyful.
  - Mailboxes sprout around the stoop, each popping confetti.
  - Collar projects celebratory emojis.
  - Tail forms heart-shaped arcs.
  - Pup wears a tiny party hat.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. BuddyPup trots between mailboxes, tapping each with its paw.
  2. Confetti bursts out, forming mini hearts.
  3. Pup spins with the party hat askew, laughing.
  4. It poses proudly before visiting the next mailbox.

### Runaway State — "Empty Street"
- **Title:** Empty Street
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Colors fade to dusk tones; mailbag swings sadly.
  - Pup walks away down a foggy street lined with dim lampposts.
  - Chat bubbles pop silently.
  - Tail becomes a faint line trailing behind.
  - Eyes glance back once.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. BuddyPup pauses, looking at the viewer with regret.
  2. It turns and walks into the fog, mailbag rustling.
  3. Lampposts flicker out sequentially as it passes.
  4. Only footprints remain before the loop restarts.

### Death State — "Stamped Keepsake"
- **Title:** Stamped Keepsake
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Pup turns into an embossed stamp affixed to the mailbag.
  - Fur patterns preserved in relief; eyes closed peacefully.
  - Mailbag becomes a memorial pedestal.
  - Collar projects a constant soft glow.
  - Floating postcards gently orbit.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. BuddyPup’s body dissolves into glowing ink.
  2. Ink presses onto the mailbag, forming the stamp.
  3. Postcards align around the bag like a halo.
  4. Glow pulses once before resetting.
