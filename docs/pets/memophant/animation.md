# CosmoBond Memophant Animation Plan — Gemini GIF Workflow

## Production Approach
- **Generator:** Google Gemini video diffusion tuned for gentle watercolor shading and floating stationery props.
- **Export guardrails:** 438 × 438 px, 4-second loops, parchment beige (#0F0C09) background, and slight top-down camera to show note cards.
- **Post-processing:** Convert to 60 fps PNG stacks, enhance ink bleed in Photoshop, and package GIF/WebM plus sprite sheets.

## Pet Model Snapshot
- Mini elephant with pastel lavender skin, oversized ears shaped like sticky notes, and trunk tipped with a fountain-pen nib.
- Palette: lavender body, mint inner ears, and copper tusk caps; cheeks blush peach.
- Face: wide thoughtful eyes, gentle smile, and freckles across the forehead.
- Accessories: messenger satchel overflowing with cards, floating checklists, and a tiny desk lamp hovering overhead.
- Positioned at the lower center, trunk extending toward the complication strip.

## Interaction Map
- **Note Delivery:** Triggered when the user captures a note or voice memo, showcasing writing animations.
- **Review Celebration:** Fires when reminders are cleared or tasks reviewed, highlighting joyful paper tosses.

## Animation States

### Idle State — "Desk Hover"
- **Title:** Desk Hover
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Memophant sits on a floating clipboard; satchel rests beside.
  - Ears gently flap, resembling post-it corners.
  - Desk lamp casts warm glow cycles.
  - Floating checklists flip slowly.
  - Trunk idly traces loops in the air.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Elephant inhales, ears flaring slightly.
  2. It taps the clipboard with the pen-tip trunk, producing a tiny ink dot.
  3. Satchel strap shifts as the pet adjusts posture.
  4. Ears settle back, repeating the calm hover.

### Interaction State — Note Delivery "Ink Trail"
- **Title:** Ink Trail
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Colors brighten; eyes focused.
  - Trunk extends like a fountain pen, glowing teal.
  - Floating cards align in front of the pet.
  - Lamp intensifies, spotlighting the writing area.
  - Ears fold forward to mimic paper tabs.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant unfurls the trunk, writing cursive on a card.
  2. Ink trail sparkles, forming icons representing the memo.
  3. It stamps the card with a copper tusk seal.
  4. Card floats into the satchel, resetting the stack.

### Interaction State — Review Celebration "Card Confetti"
- **Title:** Card Confetti
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Palette shifts to pastel rainbow; eyes joyful.
  - Satchel pops open, releasing miniature checkmarks.
  - Trunk curls into a party horn shape.
  - Ears flutter rapidly.
  - Desk lamp spins, casting dancing light beams.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant blows through its trunk, shooting checkmark confetti.
  2. It tosses cards into the air with both ears.
  3. The elephant catches a card on its trunk, twirls, and bows.
  4. Cards settle back neatly as it prepares to celebrate again.

### Sick State — "Overdue Burdened"
- **Title:** Overdue Burdened
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Colors dull to grayish lavender; eyes droop.
  - Satchel bulges, spilling overdue slips.
  - Desk lamp flickers weakly.
  - Trunk drags along the clipboard, leaving messy ink smears.
  - Ears droop heavily.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant struggles to lift the satchel, wobbling.
  2. It sighs, trunk smearing ink accidentally.
  3. Cards fall everywhere; the elephant scrambles to pick them up.
  4. Exhausted, it slumps, only to repeat the frazzled attempt.

### Happy State — "Archive Glow"
- **Title:** Archive Glow
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Skin glows softly; ears shimmer mint.
  - Satchel emits a golden aura; cards stack perfectly.
  - Desk lamp becomes a radiant lantern.
  - Floating badges show "Done" stamps.
  - Trunk spins a pen flourish.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant raises a stack of completed cards, hugging them proudly.
  2. It releases the cards, which orbit like planets.
  3. The elephant performs a graceful trunk flourish, leaving light trails.
  4. Cards reassemble into a neat archive box before the loop restarts.

### Runaway State — "Paper Trail"
- **Title:** Paper Trail
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Body becomes semi-transparent; ink lines sketch its outline.
  - Cards cascade downward forming a path leading off-screen.
  - Satchel slips from the shoulder, scattering notes.
  - Lamp dims to a distant glow.
  - Eyes look back sadly.
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant drops a bundle of cards that flow like a river.
  2. It steps onto the paper trail, trunk waving goodbye.
  3. The satchel slides off, landing near the viewer.
  4. Elephant fades into the distance as papers swirl, then reset.

### Death State — "Memory Capsule"
- **Title:** Memory Capsule
- **Pet Visual Description:**
  - Elephant curls around a glowing capsule filled with preserved cards.
  - Body takes on a porcelain finish with gold cracks (kintsugi style).
  - Ears fold gently over the capsule; eyes closed peacefully.
  - Desk lamp morphs into a candle emitting steady light.
  - Floating text reads "Remember".
- **Action Sequence:**
  1. Memophant exhales, sealing the capsule with a soft light ribbon.
  2. Gold cracks shimmer as the porcelain body stabilizes.
  3. Candle flame flickers once, casting long shadows.
  4. Scene holds quietly before rewinding to the sealing breath.
